Hi, I'm Timothy, a third year UC Berkeley who is passionate about Computer Science. When I first came into college as a freshman, I was a chemistry major hoping to impact the world through the realm of the natural sciences. However, through tedious labs and unenjoyable lectures, I quickly realized that chemistry was not the field for me. Taking a leap of faith I switched into the Computer Science realm, taking my first ever computer science class with no prior experience in coding. I loved it. Then, after three grueling semesters of hard work and endurance, I declared the Computer Science major. Since then, I have continued to venture in the Computer Science world: from making a Tic-Tac-Toe solver to a Recipe Book application, I have delved into various kinds of projects. Now, as I continue on and follow my passions, I hope to impact the world in more significant and lasting ways!
